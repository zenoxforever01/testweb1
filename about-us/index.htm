About

MRIDUL VERMA

Your Surat Newborn Photographer

Hey there! I am so glad you’re here and I look forward to creating amazing memories for you. I specialize in newborn, baby and maternity photography, serving the southeast Michigan and Metro Detroit area. My studio is located in Taylor, Michigan. 

My photography journey started over 10 years ago when I had my two girls. I wanted photos of them that were a little bit more artistic and creative, so I started learning newborn photography and I fell in love with it. Since then I’ve trained with some of the best in the industry. I’m very comfortable handling and posing newborns safely, I’ve photographed hundreds of babies, so you can be sure that your little one is in good hands. I love this job so much, being able to create these precious memories for my clients is so rewarding.
